Blog Header 1

It all started in a basement. I just turned 24, and once again had to tell Shariq has was right about something. I told him he was right about Treehouse being a pretty awesome platform to learn how to code. How do we get started? He was already over a thousand points ahead of me in his one web design lessons (and even built an Android app). I was impressed. Instead of playing video games (although that certainly still does happen), we found ourselves picking up HTML and then CSS and then he moved towards Javascript and I picked up to Adobe Creative Suite to breathe some aesthetics into the ideas for websites we talked about over beers.
Shaikh Sites is our first website, but will certainly not be our last. It is the culmination of months of bringing our newly learned skills together to built a helpful resource for web designers and developers both amateur and in their intermediacy. So thank you for visiting this site, and stay tuned for more updates and additions to this growing online network. Thanks!
